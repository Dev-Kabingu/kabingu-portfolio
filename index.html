who am i

Hi, I’m Mercy Kabingu, a designer and developer with a passion for creating great user experiences. I specialize in UI/UX design, web development, and data visualization, and I love turning ideas into clean, functional designs that are both user-friendly and visually appealing. I have experience working with a range of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, Python, and PHP, so I’m comfortable building everything from simple websites to more complex web applications. Whether I’m working on the design or the code, I’m always focused on delivering solutions that are both practical and enjoyable for users.
